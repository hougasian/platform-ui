How do I generate multiple face amounts?
You can generate up to 5 quotes with different face amounts at a time. This means that the combination of the min/max and the increment can only add up to a total of 5. For example, with a Min of 10,000, a Max of 30,000 and an Increment of 5,000 you get a set of 5 quotes. In this example, Max of 40,000 would be too high, while an Increment of 3,000 would be too low. If you are not within the allowed amounts, the form will notify you when you submit.
